01 / About Me

Where Science
Meets Sustenance

I'm a final-year Food Technology student at Aligarh Muslim University, with hands-on industry experience at ITC Limited's Haridwar manufacturing plant. My work sits at the intersection of quality systems, food safety compliance, and product development.

During my training at ITC, I led a packaging quality improvement project targeting leakage reduction and infestation risk control — applying FMEA and Root Cause Analysis to eliminate real defects on the production line.

Beyond the shop floor, I'm developing fibre-enriched gluten-free cookies using sorghum and chiku peel flour — exploring how underutilized ingredients can create functional foods for diverse dietary needs.
